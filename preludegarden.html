Immerse yourself in a serene world, made in Unity in collaboration with Liminal VR, Relax amidst stunning flora, interact with musical plants, and unleash your creativity through immersive music creation. Find solace, inspiration, and tranquility in this enchanting VR experience.

Here are some notable contributions that I made:

  • Developed the art direction and visual style, considering the unique requirements of Oculus Quest.
  • Led a team of artists and designers to the plants, environment, and visual identity.
  • Developed various non-organic 3D models, adding depth and realism to the virtual world.
  • Created captivating VFX and particle systems to enhance the visual impact of the experience.
  • Crafted interactive sound effects (SFX) for engaging with the plants in Prelude Garden.

As the Art Director for Prelude Garden, I take immense pride in achieving exceptional visual quality despite the requirements, time constraints, and limitations of the Oculus Quest platform.
