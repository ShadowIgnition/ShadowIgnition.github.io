ABOUT ME

Hi, My name is Dylan,

I'm a Passionate Game Developer specializing in Programming and Design, when I'm not working you can often find me experimenting with side projects, playing and analyzing video games or occasionally doing some 2D & 3D Art.

With an interest in systematic game design and mechanics, my dream game would be a Turn-Based Tactics game similar to the Final Fantasy Tactics or Tactics Orge Series with a heavy focus on Systematic Gameplay and mechanics.
